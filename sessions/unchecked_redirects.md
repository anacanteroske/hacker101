Unchecked Redirects
===================

In this session we'll discuss unchecked redirects.  These make it easy to mask phishing attempts and can even allow violation of authorization constraints.

What you'll learn
-----------------

- Unchecked Redirects
	- What they are
	- Detection
	- Exploitation
	- Mitigation
